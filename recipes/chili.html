Chili

Recipe

My father and I created this easy 5-ingredient chili recipe and everyone we have made it for loved it. It is fast, simple, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Kidney beans
  • Chili beans
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet
  2. Cook and stir ground beef
  3. Stir in beans
  4. Stir in tomate sauce and add seasoning
